Why I Created The Good Part

Planning a wedding should feel exciting — not overwhelming. After planning my own intimate wedding, I realised how many couples just want a celebration that feels relaxed, personal and meaningful.

The Good Part exists to help couples ignore the pressure, focus on what actually matters, and create weddings that feel truly like them.

Not Every Couple Wants a Traditional Wedding

Some couples want the big ballroom, the rigid schedule and every tradition. But many don’t.

Many couples want something calmer. More personal. A wedding that feels like a meaningful celebration rather than a production.

That’s where The Good Part comes in. I help couples focus on the atmosphere, the people and the moments that actually matter.
